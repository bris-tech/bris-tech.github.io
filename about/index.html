About

Bristech is a group of technically-minded people meeting up once a month for beer, pizza and some (hopefully) informative talks about new and interesting things that can be done in the world of computer programming.

We are not fussy about your language of choice, nor your professional background - we believe that there's something to learn from everything across the spectrum of programming. So whether you're a PHP guru, a C# enterprise developer, a Haskell researcher or an Arduino hobbyist, if you've got something interesting to tell us about, why not come and give us a talk?
