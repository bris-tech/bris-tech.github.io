Monthly meetups

We're not fussy about your language of choice, nor your professional background - we believe that there's something to learn from everything across the spectrum of programming. So whether you're a PHP guru, a C# enterprise developer, a Haskell researcher or an Arduino hobbyist, if you've got something interesting to tell us about, why not come and give us a talk?

    Conferences

    In 2015, the first Bristech conference was held at At Bristol with over 200 delegates attending 3 streams of high quality talks from across a wide spectrum of Software Engineering topics.

    The Bristech Conferences are by developers, for developers - we're all keen software engineers that firmly believe that there are interesting things to learn from all of our colleagues, whether they work in the same space or with the same languages as we do, or not.

    In 2016, we'll be returning to At Bristol for the next conference - watch this space.
